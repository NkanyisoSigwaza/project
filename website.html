Absa Business Cards Overview

1. Visa Corporate and Purchasing Cards

Visa Corporate Card

Benefits:

  • Customizable spending limits for better control.
  • Detailed expense reports for reconciliation.
  • Reduces reliance on petty cash, enhancing security.

Fees:

  • Monthly Fee: R100 per card (subject to change based on usage).
  • Transaction Costs: Standard POS purchase fees apply.

Limits:

  • Spending limits set by the organization.

Qualifying Criteria:

  • Medium to large enterprises.
  • Corporate banking account with Absa required.
  • Must meet Absa’s corporate credit risk assessment.

Additional Notes: A versatile card for organizations with multiple employees requiring controlled expense management.

Visa Purchasing Card

Benefits:

  • Streamlines procurement, reduces paperwork.
  • Offers detailed transaction-level reporting.

Fees:

  • Monthly Fee: R100 per card.

Limits:

  • Tailored spending limits based on purchasing needs.

Qualifying Criteria:

  • Aimed at businesses requiring streamlined purchasing processes.
  • Corporate banking account with Absa required.

Additional Notes: Ideal for businesses with frequent procurement needs.

2. Business Credit Cards

Business Revolve Credit Card

Benefits:

  • Revolving credit for short-term cash flow needs.
  • Flexible repayment terms based on cash flow.
  • Statements with categorized expenses for tracking.

Fees:

  • Annual Fee: R750 per card.
  • Interest Rate: Prime + 2%.

Limits:

  • Customized based on business financial performance.

Qualifying Criteria:

  • Businesses requiring access to short-term credit.
  • Requires financial documentation and proof of income.

Additional Notes: Suitable for businesses that need ongoing credit without reapplying for loans.

Business Silver Card

Benefits:

  • Lower credit limit, ideal for small businesses.
  • Helps businesses establish a credit record.
  • Simplified approval process for startups.

Fees:

  • Annual Fee: R300 per card.
  • Transaction Fee: R5 per purchase.

Limits:

  • Credit limit ranges from R5,000 to R25,000.

Qualifying Criteria:

  • Small businesses or startups.
  • Requires proof of business registration and income.

Additional Notes: A great option for new businesses seeking a basic credit facility.

Business Gold Card

Benefits:

  • Higher credit limits and premium benefits.
  • Travel insurance for business trips.
  • Premium account management and tailored reports.

Fees:

  • Annual Fee: R1,200 per card.
  • Interest Rate: Prime + 1%.

Limits:

  • Credit limit ranges from R25,000 to R250,000.

Qualifying Criteria:

  • For medium to large businesses with higher turnover.
  • Requires documentation for financial performance.

Additional Notes: Best for businesses with frequent large transactions.

3. Virtual Pay Cards

Virtual Card

Benefits:

  • Highly secure with Virtual Card Numbers (VCNs).
  • Ideal for online payments and e-commerce.
  • Reduces reliance on physical cards for transactions.

Fees:

  • Monthly Fee: R50 per card.
  • Transaction Fee: R2 per transaction.

Limits:

  • Customizable based on purchasing needs.

Qualifying Criteria:

  • Requires an active Absa business account.
  • Must use Absa’s integrated payment systems.

Additional Notes: Ideal for businesses prioritizing online security.

4. Visa Procurement Cards

Travel Lodge Card

Benefits:

  • For travel-related expenses, including accommodation and transport.
  • Integrates with corporate travel programs.

Fees:

  • Monthly Fee: R100 per card.

Limits:

  • Tailored based on travel needs.

Qualifying Criteria:

  • Requires travel bookings via Absa’s travel partners.

Additional Notes: Ideal for businesses with frequent travel needs.
